Minh monthly deposits the same amount of money (Dong) into a savings account. Interest will be added to account at the end of each month (not round).

 He wants to receive N (Dong) after M months. Calculate the amount of money Minh should deposit into his account.

Input

The first line contains 2 integers N, M (0 < N ≤ 1015, 0 < M ≤ 1000).

Output

The required amount of money (round to 4 decimals places). (Allow relative error of 10-2)

Input

Output
